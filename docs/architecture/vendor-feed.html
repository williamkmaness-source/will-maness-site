Vendor Feed in progress

Slug: vendor-feed  ·  Preview: willmaness.com/work/vendor_feed  ·  Route: /work/vendor_feed  ·  Repo: will-maness-site

Overview

An automated vendor and market intelligence pipeline. Scrapes public pages from target vendor companies, extracts structured entity data (product announcements, pricing changes, executive moves), deduplicates across sources, and surfaces a curated feed on the site.

Currently in MVP development. The goal is 30+ entities, 5+ companies, and 3+ successful pipeline runs before unlocking post-MVP features. The site shows a preview of the feed at /work/vendor_feed.
